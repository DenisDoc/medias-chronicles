Înființarea Localității Mediaș

Prima mențiune documentară a așezării. Documentele istorice atestă formarea nucleului urban care va deveni unul dintre cele mai importante centre comerciale și culturale ale Transilvaniei medievale.

Saxon Colonization & Urban Growth

The systematic arrival of German settlers begins to shape the urban structure, introducing new agricultural techniques, crafts, and the distinct fortified architecture that would define the region.

Attestation of Terra Medies

Terra Medies' first documentary attestation appears in a royal diploma issued by King Stephen V of Hungary, granting specific privileges and rights to the settlement's inhabitants.

Rise of the Ecclesiastical Center

The settlement expands around the central St. Margaret's Church. This period marks the beginning of significant stone construction and the establishment of powerful craft guilds that would dominate the city's economy.

1359
XIV

Elevation to Civitas Mediensis

A pivotal moment in history: Mediaș is officially granted the status of "Civitas" (City). This royal decree acknowledges its economic power and grants the right to build full fortification walls and hold annual markets.
